# FocusFlow+

AI-Based Context-Aware Focus and Productivity Assistant

## Overview
FocusFlow+ is a mobile application designed to help individuals improve their focus, manage their time effectively, and complete tasks with greater efficiency.
The application adapts to the user’s current situation by considering the type of task, their energy level, and the time available. Based on this information, it provides personalized guidance to structure work sessions in a way that maximizes productivity while reducing fatigue.

## Purpose
Many individuals struggle with maintaining focus and organizing their time when working on tasks. Traditional productivity tools often rely on fixed timers or rigid schedules that do not reflect the user’s actual condition or needs.
FocusFlow+ aims to provide a more adaptive and user-centered approach by offering flexible focus plans that align with the user’s current context.

## What the Application Does
* Helps users organize their work into structured focus sessions
* Recommends appropriate break durations and simple recovery activities
* Provides short motivational messages to encourage consistency
* Promotes a balanced approach between work and rest

## Expected Impact
FocusFlow+ is intended to support users in developing better work habits, improving concentration, and reducing mental fatigue. By adapting to each user’s situation, the application encourages a more sustainable and effective way of completing tasks.
